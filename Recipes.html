Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A traditional Italian pasta dish that's simple yet elegant.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti until al dente
  2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta until crispy in a large pan
  3. Beat eggs with grated cheese and black pepper in a bowl
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
  5. Add hot pasta to pancetta pan, remove from heat
  6. Pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing quickly while adding pasta water to create creamy sauce
  7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy cookies loaded with chocolate chips - a timeless favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 225g butter, softened
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 280g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 340g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cream butter and both sugars until fluffy
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract
  4. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in separate bowl
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
  7. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden
  9. Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire rack

Vegetable Stir Fry

Quick, healthy, and bursting with fresh flavors and colorful vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir fry for 30 seconds
  3. Add harder vegetables first (broccoli, carrots), stir fry for 3-4 minutes
  4. Add bell pepper and snap peas, continue stir frying for 2 minutes
  5. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch in a small bowl
  6. Pour sauce over vegetables and toss until coated
  7. Serve hot over rice or noodles
