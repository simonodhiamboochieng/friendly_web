Fluffy Blueberry Pancakes

15 min prep 15 min cook Serves 4
View Ingredients & Instructions
Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Pinch of salt
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in a separate bowl; combine with dry ingredients until just mixed.
  3. Gently fold in blueberries.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium, add a small knob of butter. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden. Serve warm with maple syrup.

Chicken Avocado Wrap

10 min Serves 2
View Ingredients & Instructions
Ingredients
  • 2 large tortillas
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • Handful mixed greens
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise or yogurt
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper
Instructions
  1. Toss sliced chicken with lemon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread mayo/yogurt on each tortilla, layer greens, chicken, and avocado.
  3. Fold and roll tightly. Slice in half and serve immediately.
